FROM CEO'S DESK

I am very pleased to welcome you to Paudel & Co Bank. Please explore and see yourself what we have to offer.

Now is the most exciting yet challenging time at Paudel & Co Bank as it continues to pursue lofty expansion plans. I feel very humbled and happy to take the lead in this challenging pursuit of growth and expansion. Together with the support of our energetic people, dedicated Board members, loyal customers and reliable suppliers, I believe we will accomplish our goals of becoming the most preferred bank among customers of all strata.

The Banking sector is one of the most dynamic and fast growing sectors in the country with a lot of challenges and competition. We are well equipped and well-resourced to compete with wide array of services and products. That being said, we are committed to operate with the highest ethical standards and transparency in our all of businesses. Our customers are our biggest asset and we are passionate about providing the most satisfying experience to them. We are equally committed to becoming a responsible member of our community and environment where we promote environmentally sustainable practices, create jobs and opportunities for community and contribute to the economic prosperity of the country.

I would like to thank all the stakeholders for their continued support and loyalty, who along with our dedicated employees drive the growth and success of our Bank. We are striving to sustain and build on the remarkable success which we have enjoyed after series of mergers and acquisitions. Our focus is to challenge the competitive market; working proactively with our stakeholders to find the best solutions to maintain sustainable long term relationships.
