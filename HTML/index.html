About Paudel & Co Bank

Paudel & Co Bank., gone through various phases in the financial sector since 10 years going trajectory over a short period of its existence, has got its own premises at Bansthali. The growth of Paudel & Co Bank was phenomenal, especially after merge of ABC Bank Ltd, Baniya Cooperative Ltd, Shrestha Samik Bank Ltd, Yadav & Co Bank Ltd, Acharya & Prajan Ltd and Shakya & Tradings Finance in 2016, attaining the status of "A" class financial institution licensed by the central bank of Nepal. Nepal Rastra Bank.

Paudel & Co Bank, completing years of journey since the inception and has got accommodated Six different financial institutions in its making of "A" class. The bank has got a network of 50 branches and 75 ATM across the country and 10 ATM across India, making it a premier private bank in terms of geographical reach and clientele segments with customer base of 175,000 among the provate sector commercial banks in Nepal. The bank is leading the global market of financial services in Nepal, the bank will be there where growth is, connecting customers and merging with other banks making it one and only Lion in the field of financial bank in Nepal. The bank provides full range of banking and investment services for personal and corporate customers, backed by the team of highly skilled, motivated, young and dynamic professionals.

Personalized service, prompt transactions, fund transfer, bill payment service, internet banking and portfolio management are few of the core strength of the bank. It has been undertaking activities such as variety of deposit schemes, loans and advances, Foreign exchange facilities, Trade financing, Inward/outward remittances, market maker for government securities, Non-Fund based service like issussing letter of credits, guranrees etc.

While ensuring adequate finance is a vital part in the setting up and runing of any business, it alone does not gurantee success. Success depends on the other factors such as quality consulation and counseling. So the bank has given due priority in developing human resources capable enough to understand the need of customer and meet their requirements. The bank keep its customer at the centre of all it does, making all the transactions, profit and loss transparent. Paudel & Co Bank believes that a bank is only successful when its customers are successful.
